Course Modules

🧩

Module 1: Fitness Assessment & Goal Setting

  • Comprehensive body type and fitness level assessment
  • Measuring BMI, flexibility, and endurance capacity
  • Personalized goal setting (weight management, strength building, or improved mobility)
  • Understanding body balance, breathing techniques, and baseline measurements
🧘‍♂️

Module 2: Warm-Up & Stretching Techniques

  • Dynamic and static warm-up routines for safe exercise
  • Safe stretching practices for full-body mobility enhancement
  • Joint activation exercises and injury prevention strategies
  • Daily 10-minute flexibility routine you can practice independently
💪

Module 3: Strength & Core Training

  • Beginner to intermediate bodyweight workouts
  • Core strengthening exercises (planks, bridges, leg raises)
  • Resistance and balance training for stability
  • Weekly progress tracking with increased difficulty levels
🩰

Module 4: Posture Correction & Body Alignment

  • Identifying and correcting poor posture habits
  • Targeted exercises for back, shoulder, and neck alignment
  • Standing, sitting, and walking posture training
  • Practical daily posture maintenance routine
🏃

Module 5: Cardio & Endurance Building

  • Light cardio training (walking, jogging, skipping)
  • Breathing rhythm synchronization and stamina improvement
  • Interval training techniques for effective fat burning
  • Home-based cardio alternatives for all weather conditions
🥗

Module 6: Nutrition & Healthy Lifestyle Habits

  • Basic understanding of nutrition and proper hydration
  • Balanced meal planning for sustained energy throughout the day
  • Sleep quality, stress management, and daily discipline techniques
  • Avoiding unhealthy patterns and building long-term sustainable habits
🧠

Module 7: Mind-Body Connection & Motivation

  • Understanding the crucial link between mind and physical movement
  • Building focus, patience, and consistency in training
  • Evidence-based motivation strategies for sustainable progress
  • Weekly reflection sessions and self-assessment techniques
🏆

Module 8: Personalized Plan & Final Evaluation

  • Creation of customized home exercise plan for long-term use
  • Performance tracking tools and comprehensive feedback session
  • Evidence-based lifestyle recommendations for sustained health
  • Certificate of completion and ongoing support options
